Save Your Crew

Your crew mates were ejected into space by an explosion. Save them with your net launcher before it is too late!

A simple shooter, but it is the very first game I have made.

Save Your Crew – Game Play

Developing Save Your Crew

Developed over 4 weeks by a 5-person team, this project was built in C++ on the cProcessing Engine. It was the first game I have ever made, and despite being a simple shooter, I am still proud of the game that we made as a team. It taught me a lot working in a team and laid the foundation for building other games.
